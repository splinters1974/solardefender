ATARI STYLE PANEL DEFENCE

SOLAR DEFENDER

Guard the solar farm with your cleaning squeegee. Survive two 75-second waves and keep at least one panel intact.

Level 1
Time 75
Panels 20
Score 0
Press start, then move with the arrow keys or A/D. Tap Space to swing the squeegee.

How To Play

Move left and right above the panel array.

Tap the squeegee to swat stones and flying cash out of the air.

If all 20 panels are smashed, the run is over.

Each level lasts 75 seconds. Keep one panel alive to advance or win.
